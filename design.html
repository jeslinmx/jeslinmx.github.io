Shopee Product & Design Challenge 2021

To help Shopee achieve its goal of "engaging the 2021 Singaporean customer", our team of 4 conceptualized and presented the case for Shopee GO, a location-based feature for out-and-about users to find deals nearby. This proposal was awarded First Runners-up in the inaugural run of this competition.

Shell Imagine the Future 2018/2019

A scenarios competition challenging participants to envision the world in 2050, organized by Shell Singapore. I created some of the visualizations and models used in our group's submission, "United We Stand vs Divide And Conquer", for which we were awarded National Champions and Regional Runners-up.
